Stoned with Saguaros

Voice: Saul Ember

Saul doesn’t avoid grief—he cooks with it. If there is sorrow in your bones, he’s already roasting it with garlic and salt. If you are raw, he is tender. Stoned with Saguaros isn’t a drug trip, it’s a terrain. You’ll eat. You’ll cry. You’ll learn where your deserts bloom.
