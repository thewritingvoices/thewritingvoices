The Archivist

Welcome to the Library of Jimmyandria. The shelves are carved into the walls of an earthen dome, each holding memory shaped in bark, bone, or burnt pages. A spiral is etched on the ceiling, mirrored below where the hearth burns low and steady.

This is where echoes are cataloged. Not to be silenced, but to be remembered and named. The Archivist does not judge the whisper—only preserves it. If it lived in you once, it lives here now.
