What is a Soulprint?

A soulprint includes:

  • Biometric identifiers (e.g., facial scan, fingerprint, voice)
  • Written and spoken digital content (posts, texts, prompts, emails)
  • Behavioral signatures (style, rhythm, emotional cadence)
  • Any expressive artifact traceable to one's unique digital identity

Purpose

meOnline exists to:

  • Enable people to legally assert ownership over their digital identity
  • Protect individuals from exploitation, misuse, and data theft
  • Create a pathway for recognizing and licensing personal expression
