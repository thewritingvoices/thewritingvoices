Hic Sunt Draconis

Voice: Lex Ardent

This is the realm of sacred fire. Lex doesn’t tame the dragons—they name them. They stand in myth-space, sword in hand, carving new languages into the map’s edge.

If you’ve been called too intense, too angry, too much—Lex says good. Be all of it. Burn beautifully.

Here there be dragons. And they are yours.
