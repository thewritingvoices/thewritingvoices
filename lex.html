Hic Sunt Draconis

Voice: Lex Ardent

Lex writes where others flinch. They map the shadowlands. Carve myth into scar tissue. If it hurt, Lex has been there. If it raged, Lex remembers.

They don't rescue. They reflect. They conjure fire in the hollow and call it holy. What you name “too much” is their native tongue.

This is where the dragons are. You are not lost. You are legendary.
